# Transit AI Knowledge Base

Predictive analytics in public transit can help agencies forecast ridership, trip runtime, service delays, and vehicle crowding.

Machine learning models can support route planning by identifying demand patterns across routes, time periods, weather conditions, and service areas.

Explainable AI is important in transit decision-making because operational teams need to understand why a model makes a prediction.

Data governance is necessary when transit systems use passenger, vehicle, GPS, and operational data. Governance helps ensure privacy, quality, accountability, and responsible use.

Simulation tools can use prediction outputs to test service scenarios, including new routes, schedule changes, fleet allocation, and disruption planning.

Weather, traffic, special events, and seasonal trends can affect ridership and runtime predictions.
